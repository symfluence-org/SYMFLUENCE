# SYMFLUENCE Corporate Contributor License Agreement (CLA)

## Corporate Contributor License Agreement v1.0

Thank you for your organization's interest in contributing to SYMFLUENCE
("the Project"), maintained by the SYMFLUENCE Foundation <dev@symfluence.org>.

This agreement allows an entity (the "Corporation") to authorize contributions
from its designated employees to the Project, and to grant the necessary
licenses for those contributions.

By signing this agreement, the Corporation accepts and agrees to the following
terms for all present and future contributions submitted to the Project by
designated employees of the Corporation.

### 1. Definitions

**"Contribution"** means any original work of authorship, including any
modifications or additions to an existing work, that a designated employee
of the Corporation intentionally submits to the Project for inclusion.
"Submit" means any form of electronic or written communication sent to the
Project, including pull requests, patches, issues, comments, or other means.

**"Corporation"** means the legal entity on behalf of which this agreement
is signed.

**"Designated Employee"** means any employee, contractor, or agent of the
Corporation who is authorized by the Corporation to make Contributions under
this agreement. The initial list of Designated Employees is provided in the
Schedule below and may be updated by the Corporation by written notice to the
SYMFLUENCE Foundation.

### 2. Grant of Copyright License

The Corporation hereby grants to the SYMFLUENCE Foundation and to recipients
of software distributed by the SYMFLUENCE Foundation a perpetual, worldwide,
non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable copyright license to
reproduce, prepare derivative works of, publicly display, publicly perform,
sublicense, and distribute Contributions made by Designated Employees and
such derivative works.

### 3. Grant of Patent License

The Corporation hereby grants to the SYMFLUENCE Foundation and to recipients
of software distributed by the SYMFLUENCE Foundation a perpetual, worldwide,
non-exclusive, no-charge, royalty-free, irrevocable patent license to make,
have made, use, offer to sell, sell, import, and otherwise transfer
Contributions made by Designated Employees, where such license applies only
to those patent claims licensable by the Corporation that are necessarily
infringed by a Contribution alone or by combination of a Contribution with
the Project.

### 4. Representations

The Corporation represents that:

- It is legally entitled to grant the licenses in this agreement.
- Each Designated Employee is authorized to make Contributions on behalf of
  the Corporation.
- Each Contribution by a Designated Employee is an original creation, or the
  Corporation has sufficient rights to grant the licenses in this agreement
  with respect to such Contribution.
- The Corporation is not aware of any third-party claims regarding
  Contributions made by its Designated Employees.

### 5. Designated Employee Management

It is the Corporation's responsibility to:

- Maintain an accurate list of Designated Employees.
- Notify the SYMFLUENCE Foundation promptly when a Designated Employee's
  authorization is added or revoked.
- Ensure that each Designated Employee is made aware of the terms of this
  agreement.

Contributions by individuals who are not Designated Employees are not covered
by this agreement and require a separate Individual CLA.

### 6. Dual Licensing

The Corporation acknowledges that the SYMFLUENCE Foundation may license the
Project, including Contributions made by Designated Employees, under
additional license terms beyond the GPL-3.0-or-later license, including
commercial licenses. The copyright and sublicensing rights granted in
Section 2 expressly permit this.

### 7. No Obligation

The Corporation understands that the decision to include any Contribution in
the Project is at the sole discretion of the SYMFLUENCE Foundation, and this
agreement does not create an obligation to use or include any Contribution.

### 8. Governing Law

This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws
of the Netherlands, without regard to its conflict of law provisions. Any
dispute arising under this agreement shall be subject to the exclusive
jurisdiction of the courts of Amsterdam, the Netherlands.

## How to Sign

An authorized representative of the Corporation should submit a pull request
adding the following information to the `CLA_SIGNATURES` file:

```
Corporation: [Legal Entity Name]
Address: [Corporate Address]
Authorized Representative: [Name and Title]
Email: [Contact Email]
Date: [YYYY-MM-DD]

Designated Employees:
- [Name] ([GitHub Username])
```

By submitting that pull request, the authorized representative confirms that
they have the authority to enter into this agreement on behalf of the
Corporation and that they have read, understood, and agree to the terms of
this Corporate Contributor License Agreement.
